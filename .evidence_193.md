### Known v1 quirk (noted, not blocking)
Each pool's ratings are an independent frame (the "learner" of that pool's matches), and
`register-snapshot` enters a new member at the *target* pool's learner rating — which belongs to the
opposite role's frame. Functional, but cross-pool ratings aren't comparable; a cleaner carry-over of
the learner's own rating is a natural refinement alongside the #190 matchmaking follow-up.
